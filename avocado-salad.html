Avocado Salad

A fresh and nutrient-packed salad that's easy to prepare and perfect for a healthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Nutritional Information:

Nutrient Amount per Serving
Calories 250 kcal
Protein 4 g
Carbohydrates 15 g
Fat 20 g
Fiber 7 g

Instructions:

Follow these steps to assemble your refreshing avocado salad:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Dice the avocados, chop the tomato, and slice the red onion thinly.
  2. Mix the salad: In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, avocado, tomato, and red onion.
  3. Add the dressing: Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad. Toss gently to coat.
  4. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste. Adjust dressing if needed.
  5. Serve: Enjoy immediately for the freshest flavor.
