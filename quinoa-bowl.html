Quinoa Bowl

A high-protein, filling bowl packed with healthy ingredients like quinoa, chickpeas, vegetables, and a light dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare ingredients: Cook quinoa and chickpeas. Chop vegetables and cilantro.
  2. Combine: In a large bowl, mix quinoa, chickpeas, tomato, red onion, and cilantro.
  3. Add dressing: Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve: Enjoy immediately or chill for later.

Nutritional Information:

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 350 kcal
Protein 12 g
Fat 10 g
Carbohydrates 52 g
Fiber 9 g
